Introduction to best telecom product distributor

Hidasie Telecom is borne out of the government's decision to modernize and restructure the telecom sector whereby the distribution unit of the then Ethiopian Telecommunic ations Corporation (ETC) to be undertaken by the private sector.

Based onthis Goal of the government those who donâ€™t join t he new company ethio telecom organized themselves in a share company and set up Hidasie Telecom Sh.Co in 2011 with the major objective of distributing telecom products and services..

Over the years, however, the company has diversified its products and services portfolio and now engaged in the distribution of telecom, financial and renewable energy products & services with its vast distribution channels of more 800 and 200 franchise networks across the country.


The company has employed more than 4000 citizens of which more than 1500 are sales persons. The major business partners of the company are Telecom Operators, FinTechs, Commercials Banks, Insurance Companies, Solar and Mobile Suppliers, Original electronics equipment manufacturers, assemblers, and Businesses.
